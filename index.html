About Versus Town

Versus Town is a Minetest server that combines fast-paced PvP action with deep survival mechanics. Build, battle, and rise to the top in a dynamic world full of opportunity and challenge!

Features

  • ⚔️ Intense PvP Zones
  • 🏰 Build your own faction or town
  • 🌎 Custom Maps & Biomes
  • 🎁 Loot crates & supply drops
  • 🛡️ Unique armor and weapons
  • 🚫 Anti-cheat protections

How to Join

  1. Download Minetest from minetest.net.
  2. Open the game and go to the "Join Game" tab.
  3. Enter the server address: versustown.minetest.xyz
  4. Port: 30000
  5. Click "Connect" and start playing!
