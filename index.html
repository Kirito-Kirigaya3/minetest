What is CTFG?

CTFG stands for Capture The Flag Gods. We are a competitive Minetest clan focused on teamwork, strategy, and dominating Capture The Flag gameplay. Founded by ThunderDragonZ, CTFG is known for skilled players, excellent communication, and consistent victories on popular Minetest PvP servers.

How to Join CTFG

Interested in joining the gods of Capture The Flag? Here’s how:

  1. Play actively on the servers listed above and build a solid reputation.
  2. Join our Discord community.
  3. Send a direct message (DM) to ThunderDragonZ on Discord expressing your interest.
  4. You may be invited to a trial period where your skills and teamwork will be assessed.
  5. If successful, welcome to CTFG!
